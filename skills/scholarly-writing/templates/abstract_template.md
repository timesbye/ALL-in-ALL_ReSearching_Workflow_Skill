# Abstract Template

```markdown
## Abstract

[Problem statement: 1-2 sentences describing the problem you address]

[Gap: 1 sentence describing what is missing in existing work]

[Approach: 1-2 sentences describing your proposed method]

[Key result: 1-2 sentences with the most important quantitative finding]

[Implication: 1 sentence on the broader impact or takeaway]
```

### Word count target
- Conference paper: 150-250 words
- Journal article: 200-300 words
- Thesis: 300-500 words

### Checklist
- [ ] States the problem clearly
- [ ] Identifies a specific gap
- [ ] Describes the approach
- [ ] Reports concrete results
- [ ] Stays within word limit
- [ ] Makes no unsupported claims
